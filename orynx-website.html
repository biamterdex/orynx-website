Designed and built entirely from the ground up, Maha Bot is our modular autonomous prototyping platform. It leverages a tightly integrated real-time micro-ROS bridge to coordinate heavy actuators alongside complex high-bandwidth sensor pipelines.

Equipped with low-latency WebRTC secure streaming architectures, it allows interactive teleoperation and data telemetry tracking from remote cellular networks globally.
